JOB HUNTING KNOWLEDGE BASE

RESUME TIPS:
- Tailor your resume for each job application
- Use keywords from the job description
- Quantify achievements with numbers and percentages
- Keep resume to 1-2 pages maximum
- Use a clean, ATS-friendly format
- Include a strong summary statement

INTERVIEW TIPS:
- Research the company thoroughly before the interview
- Prepare STAR method answers (Situation, Task, Action, Result)
- Practice common technical questions for your field
- Prepare 3-5 questions to ask the interviewer
- Send a thank-you email within 24 hours

NETWORKING TIPS:
- Update LinkedIn profile with keywords
- Connect with professionals in your target field
- Attend industry meetups and conferences
- Engage with company posts before applying
- Ask for informational interviews

APPLICATION STRATEGY:
- Apply to 5-10 positions per week
- Track all applications in a spreadsheet
- Follow up after 7-10 days if no response
- Customize cover letter for each application
- Apply within 48 hours of job posting

SKILL DEVELOPMENT:
- Focus on in-demand skills: Python, ML, Cloud, SQL
- Build a portfolio of 3-5 strong projects
- Contribute to open source on GitHub
- Get relevant certifications (AWS, Google, Coursera)
- Practice coding on LeetCode and HackerRank
