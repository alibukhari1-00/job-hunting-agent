--- GENERATIVE AI BASICS ---

- Generative AI creates new content (text, images, audio)
- LLMs (Large Language Models) generate human-like text
- Examples: GPT, Claude, LLaMA

--- LLM CONCEPTS ---

- Tokens are units of text
- Context window defines memory size
- Temperature controls randomness
- Top-p controls diversity

--- PROMPT ENGINEERING ---

- Clear instructions improve output quality
- Few-shot prompting provides examples
- Chain-of-thought improves reasoning
- Role prompting sets behavior

--- RAG (RETRIEVAL AUGMENTED GENERATION) ---

- Combines search + generation
- Uses vector databases (FAISS)
- Improves accuracy with external data
- Reduces hallucinations

--- EMBEDDINGS ---

- Convert text into vectors
- Used for similarity search
- Important for semantic search

--- FINE-TUNING ---

- Improves model on specific data
- Expensive compared to prompting
- Used for specialized tasks
