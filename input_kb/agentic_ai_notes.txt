--- AGENTIC AI BASICS ---

- Agentic AI systems can take actions autonomously
- Agents follow Goal → Plan → Execute → Reflect loop
- Agents use tools to perform tasks

--- COMPONENTS OF AGENTS ---

- LLM (brain of agent)
- Tools (APIs, database, search)
- Memory (short-term, long-term)
- Planner (decides next steps)

--- TYPES OF AGENTS ---

- Single-step agents
- Multi-step reasoning agents
- Tool-using agents
- Multi-agent systems

--- LANGCHAIN & FRAMEWORKS ---

- LangChain used to build AI agents
- LangGraph for workflow-based agents
- Agents can call APIs, databases, functions

--- WORKFLOWS ---

- Input → Reason → Action → Output
- Agents can loop until goal achieved
- Decision-making based on context

--- REAL-WORLD USE CASES ---

- Chatbots
- Automation systems
- AI assistants
- Task planning systems
